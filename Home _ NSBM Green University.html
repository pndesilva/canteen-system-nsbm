Student Life

NSBM offers a stellar platform for all capabilities and interests within its 45 clubs and societies, thus, musicians, sportsman, fitness maniacs, poets, artists and even techies are afforded a niche community to thrive.

Some of our programmes offer the exciting opportunity of transferring to the international university that you are enrolled in. Depending on your degree specialization and chosen university of affiliation, transfers can occur at the successful completion of first or second year.
